Rome, Italy Temple

The Rome, Italy temple was is the 162nd temple to be added to the vast list of temples owned by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. The temple was first announced on 4 October 2008 and the ground was broken on 23 October 2010. After about either years of construction, the temple was dedicated on 10 Marh 2019 by President Russel M. Nelson.
