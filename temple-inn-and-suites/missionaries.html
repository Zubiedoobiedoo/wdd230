Full-Time Missionary Services

Our brave you men and women who serve their Savior and the Church faithfully deserve a special place where they can focus on the Spirit. As such, the Temple Inn and Suites have several special services for full-time missionaries.

  • Long-term room accomodations with kitchens, full baths, and queen-sized beds
  • Dedicated office space
  • Dedicated family-history office and resources
  • Free changing rooms as needed
  • Free use of the exercise room
